France has reported Europe's highest ever number of new daily Covid cases as countries across the continent struggle to contain the Omicron variant. According to the WHO's weekly Covid update published on Tuesday, the number of new Covid infections grew by 57% in Europe in the week before 26 December, and by 30% in the Americas region.
